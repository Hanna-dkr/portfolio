I'm skilled in

Interaction design

I design intuitive interaction patterns and build scalable design systems that support clarity, consistency, and usability. My work includes auditing and refining flows, defining responsive behaviours, and creating reusable components that align user needs with technical implementation.

User research & usability testing

I’ve led, supported, and advocated for research across a range of projects, often where time and budget constraints left little room for it. This has required creativity, persistence, and strategic problem-solving—finding ways to understand user needs through informal, adaptive methods when formal support wasn’t available.

Visual design

I design clean, accessible interfaces that support clarity and usability. From typography and spacing to layout and colour, I focus on visual systems that scale—reinforcing consistency across complex products without sacrificing flexibility.

Stakeholder management

I build alignment across diverse teams by translating user needs, technical constraints, and business goals into shared priorities. From workshops to one-on-ones, I adapt my communication to different stakeholders—ensuring design decisions are understood, supported, and actionable.

Iterative design & prototyping

I use rapid prototyping to explore ideas, test assumptions, and refine interactions before development. Whether low-fi or high-fi, my prototypes are tools for alignment—helping teams gather feedback early and iterate with confidence.

Frontend handoff & dev collaboration

I collaborate closely with developers to ensure designs are clear, feasible, and accessible. With a working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I can anticipate constraints, reduce ambiguity, and support a smoother handoff—so what gets built aligns with the design intent.
